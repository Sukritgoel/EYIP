An AI based solution for automated email classification, response, routing, and straight through processing of customer queries received by service desk in organizations.

Natural language/free text though being extensively available for and within any organization is rarely used in automating and analysing key business processes. Customer support functions receive queries related to products/services through multiple channels like email, web, phone, chat etc. Due to unstructured data in these queries, these are routed to human agents who manually read, allocate and resolve queries, resulting in high labour cost and low efficiency. This slacked approach to customer service usually tampers with the firms reputation eventually affecting its business revenue. Apart from that there is high amount of data generated as reviews and blogs from social media platforms which can give key insights into the customers view of the organizations products and services. Often more than not these are lost and not best utilized in the firms interest. ENESA is a cumulative solution to this entire problem. Collaring the biggest challenge of customer email support, ENESA embedded with the latest AI and RPA capabilities can auto classify and allocate cases(consumer complaints) to the correct agent and department. It can perform straight through processing of customer requests using RPA and detect spam emails. It completely rips human intervention by auto replying to the consumer or updating certain changes within the system almost seamlessly without any supervision. Furthermore, it provides structured data to enable analytic insights on all types of data generated.


Features

  • Cognitive solution for all types of support queries
  • NLP based auto-classification and auto-response
  • Easy to configure and customize
  • Easy integration with BPM tools
  • Analytics dashboard for business insights

Benefits

  • Cost reduction through automated email workflow
  • Reduced SLA of case resolution through NLP based classification and automated response
  • Straight through processing of customer requests using RPA
  • Auto-allocation of cases to the correct agent using machine learning
  • Analytic insights due to availability of structured data
  • Improved customer satisfaction through faster query resolution
  • Easy integration with company systems using APIs and RPA

Use Cases

  • Text summarization of huge documents
  • Intelligent OCR by helping extract intent and entities out of documents
  • Chatbots to help customer support
  • Sentiment analysis on customers feedback
  • Help in hiring and recruitment process by automatically filtering the right candidates
  • Advanced filtering through blogs, websites, social media helping in social media analysis
