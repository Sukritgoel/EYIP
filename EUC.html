End user computing management

A central repository for storing all EUCs within an organization which can then be tracked, version maintained, edited and most importantly provide analytical insights on the EUCs along with calculating the risk possessed by it.

EUCs aka end user computing though informal in any organization form the backbone of many. These typically encompass any file containing strenuous, complex or even simple formulas and macros being used on a frequent basis for day to day activities. Pertaining to their highly informal nature tracking, maintaining, curbing and formalizing them is one of the biggest challenges for any organization. Most of these EUCs survive the organization for years making the author of the EUC difficult to trace and impossible to edit and evaluate for accuracy. These EUCs when participating in key business decisions often result in heavy losses to the firm if any error props up.   This is where the EUC management tool comes into play. Equipped with an array of functionalities, this tool aims to mitigate the risk posed by EUCs to an organization.   By creating a central repository for all the EUCs within the system, the tool controls the level of access granted to every individual for an EUC. Once uploaded the system creates documentation and risk scoring for every file, along within a fully customizable dashboard that briefs about all the files and their properties within the system.


Features

  • Central shared repository for all the EUCs within the organization
  • Access control for all stakeholders
  • For each EUC added:
    • Approval generation
    • Auto documentation
    • Risk scoring
    • Advanced analytics
  • Fully customizable dashboard

Benefits

  • Restriction of access to unauthorized personnel
  • Easy tracking and maintenance of EUCs
  • Business specific risk scoring algorithm
  • Powerful analytics

Use Cases

  • SOX compliant
  • End user computing remediation
  • Automation of EUCs
  • Life cycle management
