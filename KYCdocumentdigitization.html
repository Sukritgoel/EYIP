KYC document digitization

An advanced image processing technology that can extract data from scanned image of proof of identity (PoI) documents such as Passport, National ID cards, PAN card etc. with minimal training data required which otherwise is a manually intensive task.

Know your customer (KYC) is a regulatory requirement for many organizations especially the ones belonging to financial services and telecom. As part of KYC process an individuals’ details need to be verified against a physical document or proof of identity (POI) document. This verification in most cases is manual, thus consumes disproportionate time and resources. In many countries KYC may include bio-metric validation of finger print and facial recognition on POI documents. EY’s digital KYC module uses advanced image and text processing AI to automatically extract structured and comparable data from number of POI documents. Such as National ID cards, Passports, Salary Slips, Bank statements, PAN, AADHAR, Driver Licence etc. Also, the module includes capability to match and score finger print data and perform facial recognition and added features. The solution can be quickly configured for any new POI document type in case of a country/region specific process.
