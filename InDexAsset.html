InDEx - Intelligent Data Extraction

A configurable solution which can extract useful information from scanned document images like proof of identity documents, contracts, invoices, purchase orders, forms etc.

Generation of tremendous amounts of data is a part and parcel of every organization. This generated data may be available in various forms depending on the nature of the firm, including but not limited to structured content(tabular content), free text(emails), structured text(scanned pdf copies), metadata(data of all the data generated), images, handwritten material among others. This data if leveraged properly encapsulates huge business potential within. But pertaining to the complexity of structure in most forms of data, its analytical power is lost. In other cases even if structured content is present it is mostly locked within scanned pdf files or images which bring about a completely new challenge of first extracting them accurately and then performing analytical operations. EYâ€™s unremitting research in this field led to the development of InDEx, a comprehensive digitization solution capable of tackling almost all forms of data generated. With high precision it can extract data from any source provided, club it and draw powerful insights for the organization. InDEx uses cutting edge AI technologies for image processing, language understanding and pattern identification all of which help it to achieve the desired results.
