Driver Licence digitization

Driver Licence digitization is the automation of existing manual and paper-based processes, enabled by the digitization of information. The details in Driver Licence are extracted in a structured format.

Every Indian state has their own format. Our solution is scalable to multiple formats of DL document. Our solution extracts useful information from scanned DL document and convert it into structured format to make it useful for identification processes. Data collection process has been much faster than writing or typing in some cases. This speed improvement will help you to improve the duration of your processes, resulting in an improved experience for customers and users. Web scanning of document outperforms human data entry in terms of accuracy. Human error, distraction and stress can all lead to typing mistakes which can corrupt your data, and corrupt data can cost your business money. Our automate solution provides more accurate results than manual efforts.
